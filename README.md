### 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a Software Engineer specializing in **AI-powered industrial vision systems** — combining object detection, OCR, and classic computer vision to automate real-world quality-control problems. My Master's thesis (*Software Engineering for Industrial Applications*) built a hybrid **YOLOv5 + Tesseract OCR** pipeline that detects missing components on digital manufacturing templates and highlights them automatically — cutting a manual inspection step down to under a minute.

- 🔭 Currently working on **AI-based detection & highlighting systems** for industrial quality assurance
- 🧠 Interested in **object detection, OCR pipelines, and hybrid CV/AI systems**
- 🛠️ Comfortable bridging **Python AI models with C# / .NET applications** (Python.NET)
- 🌱 Exploring newer YOLO releases and adaptive/continuous learning pipelines
- 📫 Reach me on **[LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/arslanzafar-pro/)**
- ⚡ Fun fact: my thesis project can detect a missing component in a digital template in under a minute — faster than most people can find their car keys.
